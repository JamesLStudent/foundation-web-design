ABOUT THE MONARCHY

The King of Rock. The King of Electric Guitar. The Voice of Queen. Musical royalty. Each artist revolutionised music in their time. Now, thanks to the power of AI and digital hologram technology, their reign begins anew, in the studio and on the stage. We're very pleased to announce the latest and greatest group to take the world of music by storm all over again: The Monarchy.

Following in the legacy of the legendary 2012 Coachella, in which Tupac Shakur took to the stage 15 years after his death thanks to the work of an Academy Award-winning visual effects studio, these three icons are returning to perform again, but in an all-new group and with all-new music. Stay tuned.
