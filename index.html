ABOUT THE MONARCHY

The King of Rock. The Master of the Electric Guitar. The Voice of Queen. Musical royalty. Each artist revolutionised music in their time, and now they're back; the band of the century has emerged: The Monarchy. A mesmerizing collaboration of AI holograms featuring the legendary voices and personas of Freddie Mercury, Jimi Hendrix, and Elvis Presley. Together, these iconic figures from different eras have been resurrected through advanced artificial intelligence, captivating audiences around the globe with their electrifying performances. As their holographic forms shimmer on stage, the undeniable magnetism and raw talent of Mercury, Hendrix, and Presley coalesce into an otherworldly synergy, transporting spectators to an unparalleled realm of musical experience. Their familiar melodies and passionate energy leave audiences spellbound, reminding us of the indelible impact these legendary artists have had on the world of music, while igniting a new era where the past intertwines seamlessly with the future.

Following in the legacy of the legendary 2012 Coachella, in which Tupac Shakur took to the stage 15 years after his death thanks to the work of an Academy Award-winning visual effects studio, these three icons are returning to perform again, but in an all-new group and with all-new music. Stay tuned.
